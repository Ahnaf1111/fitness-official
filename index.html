FAQs

1

What are your membership options and pricing plans?

We offer a variety of membership options tailored to fit your needs, from monthly memberships to annual plans. Please visit our Membership page for detailed pricing information.

2

What amenities and facilities does your gym offer?

Our gym features state-of-the-art equipment, group fitness classes, personal training services, locker rooms with showers, and a smoothie bar.

3

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and weekends from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

4

Do you offer personal training sessions, and how can I sign up?

Yes, we offer personalized training sessions with certified trainers. You can sign up for a session by contacting our front desk or booking online through our website.

5

Can I try out your gym before committing to a membership?

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary one-day pass for new visitors. Simply visit our gym during staffed hours to get started.

6

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place at your gym?

We prioritize the health and safety of our members and staff. Our gym adheres to strict cleaning protocols, maintains social distancing guidelines, requires masks in common areas, and provides hand sanitizing stations throughout the facility.
